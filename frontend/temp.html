Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Make a reservation at our restaurant
  • Place an order for delivery or takeaway
  • Sign up for our newsletter or loyalty program
  • Participate in surveys, contests, or promotions
  • Contact us with inquiries or feedback
  • Visit our website and use our online services

Types of Information Collected

Category Examples Purpose
Contact Information Name, email, phone number, address Reservations, orders, communications
Payment Information Card details (encrypted), billing address Processing transactions securely
Preference Information Dietary requirements, seating preferences Personalizing your experience
Technical Information IP address, browser type, device information Website functionality and security
Usage Information Pages visited, time spent, interactions Improving services and user experience

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through cookies and similar tracking technologies. This includes:

  • Log information (IP address, browser type, access times)
  • Device information (hardware model, operating system)
  • Location information (general location based on IP)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  1. Service Provision: To process reservations, orders, and payments
  2. Communication: To send confirmations, updates, and respond to inquiries
  3. Personalization: To tailor our services to your preferences
  4. Marketing: To send promotional offers and newsletters (with consent)
  5. Improvement: To enhance our services, menu, and customer experience
  6. Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  7. Compliance: To meet legal obligations and regulatory requirements

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you have given clear consent for specific purposes
  • Contract: When processing is necessary for a contract with you
  • Legal Obligation: When processing is required by law
  • Legitimate Interests: When processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests

Data Retention Period

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Typically, we retain customer data for 3 years from the last interaction, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission and storage
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure server infrastructure with firewalls
  • Regular employee training on data protection
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

International Data Transfers

We store and process your data primarily in India. If we need to transfer your data outside India, we ensure adequate protection measures are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or your explicit consent.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and remember your preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies +

Required for basic website functionality. Cannot be disabled.

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Load balancing
Functional Cookies +

Remember your preferences and settings.

  • Language preferences
  • Font size settings
  • Region selection
Analytics Cookies +

Help us understand how visitors use our website.

  • Page visits and navigation
  • Time spent on pages
  • Error tracking

  • Third-Party Tools: Use browser extensions to block cookies

Your Rights & Choices

Data Subject Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal information we hold

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal information

Right to Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your personal information

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

Right to Object

Object to processing of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request. This may require you to provide additional information to confirm your identity.
